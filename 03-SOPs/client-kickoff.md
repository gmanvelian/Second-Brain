# SOP: Client Kickoff

## Information to Gather

### Platform & Technical
- Current website platform (Squarespace, Wix, Weebly, etc.)
- Domain registrar (check via WHOIS — often GoDaddy, Namecheap, or bundled with platform)
- Current POS system (Square, Clover, Lightspeed, standalone)
- POS contract status (locked in or flexible)

### Business
- Product distributors / warehouse contacts
- How product data is received (CSV, portal, API, catalog)
- Dropship arrangements
- Brands carried
- Shipping policy (nationwide, local, both)
- Current payment processor
- Sales tax considerations

### Content & Branding
- Logo file (PNG or SVG)
- Photos of physical location
- Social media accounts
- Store hours
- Team/club affiliations

### Inventory
- Does current POS track inventory?
- Want online + in-store inventory to sync?
- Multiple locations?
