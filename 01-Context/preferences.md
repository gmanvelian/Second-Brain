# Working Preferences

## Communication Style
- Short, direct responses — no long essays or hedging
- "Caveman" style: precise, minimal words
- Expand only when asked for depth
- Skip recaps of what was just said

## Work Style
- Prefers to see things working quickly, iterate from there
- Comfortable switching between Cowork and Claude Code
- Wants to understand why decisions are made, not just what to do
- Asks the same question multiple ways to make sure he understands fully

## Design Preferences
- Clean, modern aesthetic
- Follows approved prototypes closely — fidelity matters
- Prefers to get approval before building (prototype → approve → build)
