# Template: Client Info Request Email

Use this after initial approval to gather the information needed to build their site.

---

Hey [NAME],

Good news — I'm ready to start building out the new site. I've already put together a working prototype with your real product catalog and images, and now it's time to move into the real build.

**What I Need From You:**

**Distributors & Inventory**
1. Dealer account numbers and rep contacts for each brand
2. How does each distributor send you product data?
3. How do dropship orders work today?
4. Any other brands or suppliers?

**Point of Sale**
5. What register/POS system are you using?
6. Does it track inventory?
7. Is it connected to your website?
8. Are you locked into a contract?
9. Do you want in-store and online inventory to sync?

**Domain & Accounts**
10. Domain registrar login (only needed at go-live)
11. Do you have a Shopify account already?

**Shipping & Payments**
12. Ship nationwide, local pickup, or both?
13. Who handles credit card processing today?
14. Sales tax considerations?

**Branding**
15. High-res logo file (PNG or SVG)
16. Photos of the shop
17. Social media accounts to link

---

_Customize per client. The distributor and POS sections are highest priority._
